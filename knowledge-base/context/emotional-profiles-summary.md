# Emotional Profile Archetypes — Quick Summary

One-page reference for all 8 psychological archetypes used in the Stayful knowledge base.
Use when preparing for a meeting or briefing an AI system on how to respond to a specific lead.

---

## The 8 Archetypes

### 1. certainty-seeker
**What they need**: Proof, guarantees, predictability.
**Trigger phrase**: "What happens if..."
**Open rate**: 0% lost so far. Patient, responsive to detail.
**Key move**: Address every fear explicitly. Send the management agreement.
**Full file**: `by-emotional-profile/certainty-seeker.json`

---

### 2. loss-averse-switcher
**What they need**: To know the worst case is safe before they move.
**Trigger phrase**: "But my current rent is reliable..."
**Open rate**: 0% lost so far. Show the floor, not just the ceiling.
**Key move**: Side-by-side income comparison. Show January as the floor.
**Full file**: `by-emotional-profile/loss-averse-switcher.json`

---

### 3. analytical-evaluator
**What they need**: Data, numbers, written documentation.
**Trigger phrase**: "Can I see the breakdown?"
**Open rate**: 9% lost. Slowest conversion but highest loyalty.
**Key move**: Send the full document pack. Answer every question before moving on.
**Full file**: `by-emotional-profile/analytical-evaluator.json`

---

### 4. urgency-driven
**What they need**: Speed and a clear next step.
**Trigger phrase**: "How quickly can you start?"
**Open rate**: 12% lost. Highest conversion rate when energy matched.
**Key move**: Give a specific go-live date. Send agreement same day.
**Full file**: `by-emotional-profile/urgency-driven.json`

---

### 5. status-quo-resistant
**What they need**: A compelling reason to change that outweighs inertia.
**Trigger phrase**: "I'm not sure I need to change what I'm doing..."
**Open rate**: 67% lost. Highest loss rate. Needs external trigger event.
**Key move**: State the income gap clearly. Don't push. Quarterly check-in.
**Full file**: `by-emotional-profile/status-quo-resistant.json`

---

### 6. betrayal-damaged
**What they need**: To be heard, then to see specific mechanisms, not general claims.
**Trigger phrase**: "My last agent..."
**Open rate**: 0% lost so far. Requires patience. High loyalty once converted.
**Key move**: Let them tell the whole story first. Never minimise.
**CRITICAL**: Entry 016 Ihsan — brothel trauma. Extreme sensitivity. Full vetting protocol required.
**Full file**: `by-emotional-profile/betrayal-damaged.json`

---

### 7. social-proof-dependent
**What they need**: External validation from other owners or third parties.
**Trigger phrase**: "Can I speak to one of your existing owners?"
**Open rate**: 0% lost so far. Small group (3 leads).
**Key move**: Offer owner reference. Share portfolio size and Superhost status.
**Full file**: `by-emotional-profile/social-proof-dependent.json`

---

### 8. fast-path-gain-focused
**What they need**: Maximum income as fast as possible.
**Trigger phrase**: "What's the maximum I can earn?"
**Open rate**: 50% lost. High R2R overlap (all R2R abandoned).
**Key move**: Lead with income numbers. Show fee as small vs total gain.
**WARNING**: R2R operators in this archetype all abandoned. Flag immediately.
**Full file**: `by-emotional-profile/fast-path-gain-focused.json`

## Archetype Detection Quick Guide

| Signal heard | Likely archetype |
|---|---|
| Detailed damage questions | certainty-seeker |
| Asking about January void rates | loss-averse-switcher |
| Asking for the fee breakdown in writing | analytical-evaluator |
| "How quickly can we start?" | urgency-driven |
| "I'm not sure I need to change" | status-quo-resistant |
| Mentions bad experience with previous agent | betrayal-damaged |
| Asks to speak to existing owners | social-proof-dependent |
| "What's the maximum income I can make?" | fast-path-gain-focused |
