## DELAY SIGNALS — QUICK REFERENCE

### DLY-TEN: Tenant in Property
**Identifiers:** “Tenant leaving in X weeks” / “Not signing until they’re out” / “Waiting to hear from tenant”

**Best response:** “Sign the agreement now with a deferred start date — you don’t need them gone first. The offer expires before they leave if we wait.”

**Risk:** If tenant renews, the window closes permanently.

**Seen in:** entry-023 (Oliver Glaves), entry-029 (Gary Johnston), entry-030 (Shoaib Khalid)

---

### DLY-FUR: Unfurnished Property
**Identifiers:** “Still needs furniture” / “Currently empty” / “Not ready yet”

**Best response:** “Sign the agreement. Furnish after. Stayful can advise on what works best for STL — there’s a spec that maximises guest appeal and avoids over-investing.”

**Seen in:** entry-039 (Julia Norman)

---

### DLY-PUR: Purchase Not Complete
**Identifiers:** “Keys in X weeks” / “Off-plan completion [date]” / “Exchange not happened yet”

**Best response:** Sign at or before exchange. Build listing pre-completion so first booking can go in within days of key receipt.

**Competitor risk:** Developer affiliates approach at exchange. Stayful must move first.

**Seen in:** entry-003, entry-018, entry-033, entry-034, entry-041, entry-044

---

### DLY-MOV: Moving Abroad
**Identifiers:** “Moving to [country] in [month]” / “We’re relocating” / “Leaving the UK”

**Best response:** “Your relocation date is the deadline. If we start now, you’ll leave with the property already earning. If you wait, you’re leaving with an empty property and a problem to solve from [country].”

**Seen in:** entry-011 (Laura Duncker), entry-014 (Andrew McIntee), entry-038 (Noemi Konya), entry-050 (Helen Bias), entry-054 (Laura Shaw)

---

### DLY-OTH: Other Delays

**Spouse/Partner Gate** — most common DLY-OTH type
- “Wife needs to agree” / “Husband needs to be involved” / “Partner gate”
- **Response:** Don’t close. Book joint call. “Can we get them on a 20-minute call together? I can do [day].”

**Renovation** — typically 2-3 months delay
- Sign agreement now. Start date post-renovation. Stayful advises on STL-optimal finishes.

**Competitor Evaluation** — “speaking with 2 other companies”
- Make offer deadline explicit. Ask them to check competitor’s projection for cleaning cost inclusion.

## GREEN LIGHT SIGNALS — QUICK REFERENCE

### GL-SELF: Self-Motivation
| Signal | Strength | Response |
|--------|----------|----------|
| No mortgage | Critical | “Every pound of income is profit.” |
| Renters Reform concern | High | “RF reduces your protection. STL removes the tenant relationship entirely.” |
| Property already furnished | High | “We can be live in 2-3 weeks.” |
| Personal use need | Medium | “Block whatever dates you need.” |

### GL-MGMT: Management Dissatisfaction  
| Signal | Strength | Response |
|--------|----------|----------|
| <3 reviews in 6 months | Critical | “How many reviews did you get in the last 3 months? [Pause] That’s the problem.” |
| Occupancy >10pp below market | Critical | Show market benchmark vs their current figure. |
| Maintenance failures | High | Explain Stayful’s maintenance process specifically. |
| Poor comms | Medium | “Named account manager. You’ll have one person.” |

| Signal | Strength | Response |
|--------|----------|----------|

| Keys received this week | Critical | Onboarding call today. Photography this week. |
| Relocation in <8 weeks | High | Relocation date = deadline. |
| Off-plan completion <8 weeks | Medium | Sign before developer affiliate approaches. |
