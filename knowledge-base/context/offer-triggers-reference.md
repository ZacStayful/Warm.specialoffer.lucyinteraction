*Built from 48 meeting transcripts | Last updated: 2026-06-07*

---

## WHEN TO OFFER 13%+VAT

Offer ONLY when ALL of the following are true:
1. No active spouse/partner gate (both decision-makers present or confirmed agreed)
2. No product mismatch (not a guaranteed-rent seeker)
3. STL net viable (>£12k/yr)
4. At least one GL signal present
5. Property exists and is accessible within reasonable timeframe

---

## WHEN NOT TO OFFER

| Situation | Reason | Alternative |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Spouse gate active | 60%+ abandonment rate on single-party close | Book joint call instead |
| Guaranteed rent demand | Product doesn’t exist for 1-bed | Pre-qualify out |
| R2R margin <£2k/yr | Not viable | Decline gracefully |
| Renovation >3 months away | Too early | Set future touchpoint |
| Lead clearly comparing (hasn’t decided between STL/LTL) | Premature | Let projections settle first |

---

## SETTING THE DEADLINE

**Rule:** Deadline must arrive BEFORE the lead’s primary gating event.

**Examples:**
- Tenant leaving April 30 → Offer deadline April 22 √
- Tenant leaving April 30 → Offer deadline April 30 ✗ (tenant renewal = lead goes cold)
- Renovation complete July → Don’t offer yet ✗ (gate hasn’t resolved)
- Relocation September 1 → Offer deadline August 15 √

**Standard window:** 7-14 days from meeting date.
**Extension:** One extension if lead shows genuine engagement. Second extension rarely appropriate.
