# Stayful Presentation Guide v3.0
*Last updated: 2026-06-07 | Built from 48 meeting transcripts*

---

## CORE PRESENTATION PRINCIPLES

1. **Postcode-specific projections always** — generic numbers break trust before you start
2. **Post-bills net is the headline** — not gross, not pre-fee. Net monthly in the lead’s pocket.
3. **Show conservative AND realistic** — conservative case builds credibility; realistic case makes the argument
4. **LTL comparison must be fair** — include LTL maintenance costs, void periods, agent fees
5. **Avoid showing wrong-location data** — critical lesson from entry-056 (Hull lead shown West Midlands comps)

---

## SLIDE ORDER

### Slide 1: Their property
- Photo of their property (from Rightmove/Google) or placeholder
- Address, postcode, bedrooms
- Current situation (LTL tenant / vacant / purchasing / etc.)

### Slide 2: The STL market for their area
- 3 comparable Airbnb listings within 3 miles
- Their average nightly rate, occupancy, annual gross
- Source: AirDNA or live Airbnb search data

### Slide 3: Income projection
| Scenario | Occupancy | Gross/yr | Stayful fee | Bills/yr | **Net/yr** | **Net/mo** |
|----------|-----------|---------|------------|---------|-----------|----------|
| Conservative | 65% | £X | £Y | £Z | **£A** | **£B** |
| Realistic | 72% | £X | £Y | £Z | **£A** | **£B** |
| Good | 78% | £X | £Y | £Z | **£A** | **£B** |

### Slide 4: LTL comparison
| | LTL | STL (realistic) | Difference |
|-|-----|-----------------|----------|
| Monthly income | £X | £Y | **+£Z/mo** |
| Bills | Tenant’s | Owner’s (£W) | — |
| Net | £A | £B | **+£C/yr** |

### Slide 5: How Stayful works
- What’s included in 15%+VAT (or current offer %)
- Payout: 1st-5th every month
- Direct bookings: 40% of our portfolio
- Portfolio size: ~70 properties (not 700)
- Named account manager

### Slide 6: Case study (choose most relevant to lead type)
- Similar location and property type
- Show: projected vs actual performance
- Show: review growth
- Best case study for this purpose: York (entry-032 reference: £36k projected → £48k actual)

### Slide 7 (EX-STL leads only): Performance comparison
| | Current agent | Stayful (projected) |
|-|--------------|--------------------|
| Occupancy | [their %] | [target %] |
| Reviews (last 3 months) | [their count] | [Stayful benchmark] |
| Nightly rate | £X | £Y |

### Slide 8: Next steps
- Sign by [date]: 13%+VAT
- After that: 15%+VAT
- Photography booking date
- Expected live date

## CRITICAL MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Wrong-area comparables** (entry-056: West Midlands comps shown to Hull lead) — always verify postcode before meeting
2. **Gross vs gross comparison** — always compare post-bills STL vs post-maintenance LTL
3. **Omitting bills** from STL projection (same mistake developers make; mirrors their error)
4. **Unrealistic occupancy** — never open with >75% occupancy as base case
5. **Single-scenario projection** — always show conservative + realistic
6. **Forgetting to name the offer deadline** on the slide (it must be visible, not just verbal)
