# Stayful Lead Qualification Framework

A scoring guide for assessing conversion likelihood after an initial meeting.
Built from 70-lead knowledge base analysis.

---

## Qualification Score (0-100)

Score each lead after their first meeting. Use the score to determine follow-up priority and resource investment.

---

## Scoring Criteria

### 1. Property Readiness (0-25 points)

| Situation | Points |
|---|---|
| Property empty and ready to list now | 25 |
| Property available within 4 weeks | 20 |
| Tenant leaving in 1-3 months | 15 |
| Tenant leaving in 3-6 months | 10 |
| Unfurnished — needs 1-3 months work | 10 |
| Purchasing — not yet completed | 5 |
| No clear timeline | 0 |

### 2. Financial Motivation (0-20 points)

| Situation | Points |
|---|---|
| STR net is 40%+ above current LTL or mortgage | 20 |
| STR net is 25-40% above current LTL or mortgage | 15 |
| STR net is 10-25% above current LTL or mortgage | 10 |
| STR net is less than 10% above current situation | 5 |
| Lead does not know current income baseline | 3 |

### 3. Decision Authority (0-15 points)

| Situation | Points |
|---|---|
| Lead is sole decision-maker and confirmed this | 15 |
| Lead's partner is supportive but not in meeting | 10 |
| Lead needs to consult partner — partner unknown quantity | 5 |
| Multiple decision-makers, none identified | 2 |

### 4. Emotional Archetype Conversion Rate (0-20 points)

| Archetype | Points |
|---|---|
| urgency-driven | 18 |
| analytical-evaluator | 14 |
| loss-averse-switcher | 12 |
| certainty-seeker | 12 |
| social-proof-dependent | 10 |
| betrayal-damaged | 8 |
| fast-path-gain-focused | 6 |
| status-quo-resistant | 2 |

### 5. Profile Type Conversion Rate (0-10 points)

| Profile | Points |
|---|---|
| EX-STL (existing managed STR) | 10 |
| STL-SW (self-managing switcher) | 7 |
| ABROAD (overseas / remote) | 6 |
| PURCH (purchasing) | 4 |
| SELL (considering selling) | 1 |
| R2R (rent-to-rent) | 0 |

### 6. Green Light Signals (0-10 points, cumulative)

| Signal | Points |
|---|---|
| GL-TIME (ready within 2 months) | +5 |
| GL-MGMT (already with management company) | +4 |
| GL-SELF (self-managing and ready to hand over) | +3 |

## Score Interpretation

| Score | Outcome | Follow-up Priority | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| 75-100 | READY_NOW | IMMEDIATE | Send agreement within 24h. Call within 48h. |
| 55-74 | READY_SOON | HIGH | Weekly follow-up. Documents sent same day. |
| 35-54 | DELAYED | MEDIUM | Monthly follow-up. Identify specific blocker. |
| 15-34 | NURTURE | LOW | Quarterly check-in. Trigger-based re-engagement. |
| 0-14 | LOST | NONE | Close the record unless life event changes situation. |

---

## Instant Disqualifiers

These situations override the scoring model. Flag immediately.

- **R2R without landlord consent**: Do not progress without owner contract. Score becomes 0.
- **London property without planning check**: Do not proceed. 90-day rule must be verified first.
- **No clear property**: PURCH leads with no property identified score maximum 30 regardless of other factors.

---

## Qualification Questions to Score Accurately

1. When is the property available? → Property Readiness
2. What is your current rent or mortgage? → Financial Motivation
3. Is this your decision alone or do you need to discuss with someone? → Decision Authority
4. Listen for the archetype signals during conversation → Archetype
5. What is their current STR status? → Profile Type
6. Are there any green light signals present? → Green Lights
